Who We Are


â€‹Fast, efficient, and honest, Suburban Septic Service Inc has become a reputable and well-known Septic Installation, Service, and Cleaning company throughout the Northeast Ohio area. Since our founding by Patrick J. Valentine in 1987 with the purchase of one small pumper truck, the company has grown along with the Valentine Family. As a family-owned & operated company, Suburban Septic Service Inc is dedicated to providing high quality, affordable septic services to the Northeast Ohio area.

In the early 1990s, the company quickly expanded with Patrick's knowledge and experience in the wastewater treatment industry as he trained and became a licensed and registered Installer and Service Provider for multiple types of household sewage treatment systems (HSTS) approved by the Ohio Department of Health (ODH). In 1998, Suburban Septic Service Inc became a licensed distributor for multiple manufacturers of Residential Sewage Treatment Systems. Nearly 37 years later, Suburban Septic Service Inc remains one of very few companies in the region qualified to install, service, and clean your septic system.

As an Installer and Service Provider of both off-lot discharging systems covered under the NPDES General Permit as well as on-lot systems, i.e., Drip Distribution, Spray Irrigation, Sand Mound, our team has extensive knowledge and experience in the design, installation, and maintenance of systems approved by the Ohio Department of Health.
