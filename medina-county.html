Suburban Septic Service is a Family-Owned Septic Business Serving Northeast Ohio Since 1987. We specialize in: Septic Installation, Septic Repairs, Septic Cleanings, Inspections, and more. We have been a leading licensed, bonded, and insured Septic installer, service provider, and septic hauler for more than 36 years. No job is too big for our family's business.

Suburban Septic Service, your trusted septic system partner in Medina, Ohio and beyond, is committed to delivering exceptional service that goes beyond expectations. With a focus on safety, professionalism, and reliability, we've established ourselves as leaders in the septic industry.

Septic system maintenance is more than a service; it's a responsibility that requires expertise. At Suburban Septic Service, we understand the complexities of working with septage, waste materials, and the health risks they pose. That's why we're proud to be a licensed, bonded, and insured company, backed by credentials from the Ohio Department of Health. We're registered not just with the Ohio Department of Health, but also with the health departments of the counties we serve, ensuring your safety and peace of mind.

We believe that every homeowner deserves a reliable septic system. Our comprehensive range of services covers everything from maintenance and repairs to installations and emergency responses. Our team of experts is dedicated to guiding you through the process, making it simple, affordable, and efficient. With Suburban Septic Service, you're not just getting a service; you're getting a knowledgeable partner who prioritizes your wellbeing and the longevity of your septic system.
